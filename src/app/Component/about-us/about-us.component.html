Second Global SSI Multi-Speciality Robotic Surgery Conference (SMRSC) 2025

SS Innovations is delighted to announce the "Second Global SSI Multi-Speciality Robotic Surgery Conference (SMRSC) 2025," a pioneering platform to explore cutting-edge advancements in robotic surgery with the revolutionary "SSI Mantra 3" Surgical Robotic System. Building on the remarkable success of SMRSC 2024, which brought together leading minds to discuss and showcase transformative innovations, this year's event promises to elevate the dialogue further.

SS Innovations continues its commitment to transforming healthcare across specialties like Urology, General Surgery, Gynecology, Head & Neck, and Cardiothoracic surgery. Scheduled from the 7th to the 9th of March 2025, at The Leela Ambience Hotel, Gurugram, Haryana, India, SMRSC 2025 is poised to bring together an esteemed gathering of global medical experts, technologists, and visionaries. This event will spotlight the future of robotic surgery, emphasizing the advanced capabilities of the SSI Mantra 3 system. The conference will host prominent National and International Faculty, presenting scientific sessions on the evolution of robotic surgery, the role of Artificial Intelligence, and the promise of sustainable tele-surgery. Attendees will witness live surgeries performed with the SSI Mantra 3 system, showcasing its versatile applications across multiple specialties, including cardiac surgery, while gaining invaluable insights and hands-on learning opportunities. Highlighted themes include innovations in robotic surgery, enhanced capabilities of the SSI Mantra 3 system, the integration of AI in surgical practices, and the expanding potential of tele-surgery. Special focus will also be given to groundbreaking developments in multi-specialty and cardiac robotic procedures. SMRSC 2025 aspires to drive collaboration, inspire innovation, and facilitate knowledge-sharing among global thought leaders, practitioners, and industry pioneers. This conference aims to redefine the future of surgical care, with the SSI Mantra 3 system at its core. We invite you to join us for this transformative event that will shape the next chapter in robotic surgery for 2025 and beyond.
